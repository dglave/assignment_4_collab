Introduction

Data and Methods

Data for this report were collected and provided by the Santa Barbara Coastal Long Term Environmental Research site. Lobster surveys were conducted at five different sites along the mainland side of the Santa Barbara Channel: Naples, Isla Vista, Arroyo Quemado, Mowhawk, and Carpenteria. Naples and Isla Vista are contained within a Marine Protected Area; Arroyo Quemado, Mohawk, and Carpenteria are not. Divers performed four 60m x 5m transects at each site, sampling every two to four weeks through the lobster fishing season (October to March).

Size is a visual estimate of carapace length in mm.
